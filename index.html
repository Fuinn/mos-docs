MOS¶

This is the documentation for MOS, the Mathematical Optimization Service.

MOS facilitates the deployment, integration, management and utilization of mathematical optimization models. MOS makes it easier for both humans and machines to harness the power of mathematical optimization.

Models may be accessed and solved directly through the MOS REST API, or the REST API may be called indirectly through the MOS Interface Python package or Julia package, or through the MOS Frontend.

Either a user’s own models and data may be uploaded to MOS, or models and data chosen from the MOS Examples repository.
